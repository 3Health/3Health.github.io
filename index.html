About Us

3Health was created by Engineering Masters students at the W Booth School of Engineering Practice at McMaster University.

Based on conversations with individuals with arthritis and their healthcare professionals, we have identified a need for a patient-companion application that would present users with personalized information about their illnesses and symptoms and ensure that they are properly educated and are provided with the proper support and treatment.

The 3Health team has designed a mobile application that will revolutionize how a user’s illness plays a part in their life, how they interact with their rheumatologist, and the lifestyle changes and strategies that they use to best manage their respective illnesses and symptoms.

This will be your go-to app for easy exchange of valuable and personalized information between you and your healthcare professional.

You are important, so let's manage this together with 3Health.
